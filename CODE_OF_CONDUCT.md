# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive.
- Giving and receiving feedback professionally.
- Focusing on what is best for the community.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insulting, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and when an individual is officially representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers by opening a private security report or contacting maintainers through repository owner contact channels.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
